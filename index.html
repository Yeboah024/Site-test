Our Story


Great Gold Mining LLC is a Limited Liability Company based in Ghana and it was established in August, 2012 with the main purpose to provide precious metals and comprehensive logistics services to customers all over the world.


Unearthing Ghana's golden potential, Great Gold Mining delves deep into the heart of kwabeng, Atiwa West. This gold mining company leverages expertise and innovation to extract and refine precious metals, contributing to the nation's economic prosperity.


We are focused on acquisition, exploration and sourcing of Gold in an evironmentally safe method and effectively controling pollution on our mining sites and environments with the objective of providing value added services for our esteemed customers and stakeholders.


Our Company is duly registerd under the Laws of the Republic of Ghana and we have the appropraite certifications to export Gold without restrictions to any part of the world.


We are proud to announce that we have all the legally required documents to mine and export gold in Ghana and we frequently export Gold to Europe, Asia, and USA.
