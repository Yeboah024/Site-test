Our Wide Variety of Services

Great Gold Mining Services in Ghana

Gold and Diamond Export

We export your processed Gold or Diamond to your desired destination in a fast and secure manner.

Bullion Delivery

Provision of Precious Metals transportation for our high-profile Clients. We can acquire Gold from a variety of mines across Ghana (depending on the request of the buyer) and transport it to our head office for further processing. A team of top security personel will accompany any delivery to our site in a highly secure escort; this service is also guaranteed by security risk insurance.

Refining

Great Gold Mining LLC offers refining services on request. We partner with big gold refineries in Ghana to provide refining services to our customers.

Assaying

Our Company operates an ultra-modern testing laboratory. The testing methods are according to ISO/IEC 17025 standards. All sampling and testing activities are performed in compliance with the National Standards of Ghana.

Smelting

Using high quality smelting machineries both local and international, we can smelt Gold dust into bar form as required by the Laws of the Republic of Ghana.

Oil & Gas Trade

We import and re-export massive amounts of Petroleum products in barrels. Our trading performance is built on a thorough grasp of the markets, both locally and abroad, as well as our in-depth knowledge of Petroleum products, grades, demands accumulated over time.
