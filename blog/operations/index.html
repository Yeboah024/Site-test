Our Gold Operations

Precision, Integrity, Results

At Great Gold Mining LLC, our operations are built on precision, transparency, and a strong commitment to results. From responsible sourcing to efficient export logistics, every stage of our process is handled by experienced professionals and trusted partners.

We work exclusively with licensed refineries and uphold international compliance standards, ensuring that every transaction is secure and verifiable. Our gold is sourced directly from vetted and licensed mining sites and transported securely to the Sewia Refinery in Accra, Ghana—one of the most respected refineries in the region.

Once the gold arrives at the refinery, our export process begins. This includes official documentation, inspection, and preparation for shipment, all under strict quality control. Exports are handled through Emirates Sky Cargo or KLM Sky Cargo, providing international tracking and reliability. The entire process is insured, giving our clients peace of mind from start to finish.

Our operational approach is also flexible. Whether it’s a first-time buyer or a repeat partner scaling up after a successful trial, we tailor our logistics and support based on the buyer’s financial capacity and preferences. And once the gold is airborne, a valid Air Way Bill is generated so that the buyer can track the shipment in real-time.

From sourcing to export, our operations are defined by efficiency, integrity, and transparency —ensuring you receive not just gold, but a secure, seamless experience.
