Documentation and Government Clearance

Transparency and Compliance Every Step of the Way

Legal Documentation

We handle all legal paperwork on behalf of our clients to streamline the export process. Our experienced compliance team ensures each document meets international and Ghanaian regulatory requirements.

Licensing and Approvals

Every gold consignment must be approved by the Ghana Minerals Commission and the Precious Minerals Marketing Company (PMMC). We manage all interactions with these institutions and obtain the necessary clearances promptly.

What’s Included

The documentation includes gold assay reports, export invoices, export declarations, customs clearance forms, and any required certificates of origin or value.
