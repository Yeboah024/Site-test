How We Sell Gold:

Seamless, Secure & Transparent

At Great Gold Mining LLC, we offer a trusted and efficient gold trading process designed to deliver maximum assurance and satisfaction to serious buyers.

We supply 23-carat gold with a guaranteed minimum purity of 96%. The gold is delivered to the reputable Sewia Refinery in Accra, Ghana — a fully LBMA-compliant refinery — for processing and export.

The Buyer is responsible for the export and logistics cost, which is calculated at 4% of the LBMA value. Once this fee is paid, we immediately deliver the gold to Sewia Refinery, where the export process is completed in under 12 hours. We guarantee same-day export after payment confirmation.

After completion at the refinery, the gold is fully insured and shipped via Emirates SkyCargo or KLM SkyCargo. A trackable Air Way Bill (AWB) is generated, allowing the Buyer to monitor the shipment throughout transit.

A representative from our team — or the Seller personally — accompanies the gold to the Buyer’s refinery in Dubai for final assay and verification. Full payment for the gold is expected within three (3) working days after the final assay.

We begin with a trial quantity tailored to the Buyer's financial capability, and upon successful completion, we progressively increase the volume.

Buyers who wish to initiate the process may request an official FCO (Full Corporate Offer) from Great Gold Mining LLC, which includes our official stamp and seal.

All terms and conditions will be detailed in a formal Sales and Purchase Agreement (SPA), signed by both parties before the transaction begins, ensuring full legal protection and transparency.
